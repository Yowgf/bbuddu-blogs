##### Dreaming of saying "I do" somewhere unforgettable?

Destination weddings continue to grow in popularity as couples look beyond traditional venues and create experiences that combine celebration, travel, and once-in-a-lifetime memories. From tropical beaches to historic European villas, the right destination can transform your wedding into an extraordinary adventure.

Here are the 10 best destination wedding locations for 2026, along with estimated costs, ideal travel seasons, and what makes each destination special.

### 1. Bali, Indonesia

Bali remains one of the world's most sought-after destination wedding locations. Couples can choose from beachfront resorts, cliffside venues, jungle retreats, and luxury private villas.

- **Best Season:** April–October
- **Estimated Budget:** $10,000–$40,000+
- **Why Couples Love It:**
    - **Affordable luxury:** High-end private villas and 5-star service at a fraction of Western costs.
    - **Stunning scenery:** Incredible diversity from dramatic cliffside chapels to lush jungle backdrops.
    - **Experienced wedding vendors:** A highly developed wedding industry with world-class planners.
    - **Excellent honeymoon opportunities:** Seamlessly transition from a vibrant party to a relaxing tropical getaway.

### 2. Santorini, Greece

Known for its whitewashed architecture and breathtaking sunsets, Santorini is one of the most romantic wedding destinations in the world.

- **Best Season:** May–October
- **Estimated Budget:** $25,000–$80,000+
- **Why Couples Love It:**
    - **Iconic sunsets:** Breathtaking Caldera views provide an unmatched, globally recognized backdrop.
    - **Charming architecture:** Beautiful whitewashed buildings contrasting perfectly with the deep blue Aegean Sea.
    - **Boutique intimacy:** Ideal for exclusive, high-end micro-weddings and elegant terrace dinners.

### 3. Phuket, Thailand

Phuket combines beautiful beaches, luxury resorts, and affordable pricing, making it a favorite among international couples.

- **Best Season:** November–April
- **Estimated Budget:** $8,000–$35,000+
- **Why Couples Love It:**
    - **Tropical paradise:** Pristine white-sand beaches, crystal clear waters, and world-renowned hospitality.
    - **All-inclusive value:** Top-tier luxury resorts providing comprehensive wedding and guest accommodation packages.
    - **Vibrant culture:** Unique opportunities to blend traditional Thai elements, spectacular fire shows, and local cuisine.

### 4. Tuscany, Italy

Rolling vineyards, historic villas, and world-class cuisine make Tuscany an unforgettable wedding destination.

- **Best Season:** May–October
- **Estimated Budget:** $20,000–$75,000+
- **Why Couples Love It:**
    - **Rustic elegance:** Historic stone estates, cypress-lined driveways, and endless golden hills.
    - **Culinary excellence:** Famous all over the world for authentic fine dining, local olive oils, and iconic wines.
    - **Timeless romance:** A relaxed, sophisticated atmosphere that feels like a classic cinematic escape.

### 5. Kyoto, Japan

For couples seeking elegance and cultural authenticity, Kyoto offers beautiful gardens, temples, and seasonal scenery.

- **Best Season:** March–April (Cherry Blossoms) and October–November (Autumn Foliage)
- **Estimated Budget:** $15,000–$50,000+
- **Why Couples Love It:**
    - **Cultural authenticity:** Serene traditional wooden temples, Zen rock gardens, and peaceful shrines.
    - **Seasonal splendor:** Breathtaking scenery during the soft pink cherry blossom season or brilliant red autumn colors.
    - **Refined aesthetic:** Perfect for couples seeking architectural minimalism, elegant precision, and quiet serenity.

### 6. Riviera Maya, Mexico

Perfect for beach weddings, vibrant night celebrations, and effortless all-inclusive formatting.

- **Best Season:** December–April
- **Estimated Budget:** $10,000–$45,000+
- **Why Couples Love It:**
    - **All-inclusive ease:** Complete resort experiences that make guest booking and itinerary planning simple.
    - **Diverse venues:** Options range from classic white-sand Caribbean beaches to hidden, mystical jungle cenotes.
    - **Energetic atmosphere:** Rich Mayan history, festive nightlife, and endless eco-adventures for the entire guest list.

### 7. Portugal

Portugal offers rich European history, historic coastal charm, and beautiful weather without the premium prices of neighboring Western European nations.

- **Best Season:** May–October
- **Estimated Budget:** $12,000–$40,000+
- **Why Couples Love It:**
    - **Hidden European gem:** Authentic Old World architecture and coastal luxury at a highly competitive cost.
    - **Stunning landscape variety:** Sun-drenched ocean cliffs in the Algarve or magical, fairytale palaces in Sintra.
    - **Welcoming culture:** Exceptional seafood, world-renowned wines, and an incredibly warm atmosphere.

### 8. Croatia

Croatia's spectacular Adriatic coastline and medieval towns have become one of Europe's fastest-growing wedding destinations.

- **Best Season:** May–September
- **Estimated Budget:** $15,000–$50,000+
- **Why Couples Love It:**
    - **Adriatic allure:** Ancient walled coastal cities directly meeting perfectly clear turquoise waters.
    - **Island wedding dreams:** Memorable opportunities for boutique weddings on historic islands like Hvar or Vis.
    - **Dramatic historical venues:** Fortresses and historic open-air squares offering a perfect mix of history and luxury.

### 9. Maldives

The Maldives delivers unparalleled luxury, complete privacy, and postcard-perfect natural beauty for high-end celebrations.

- **Best Season:** November–April
- **Estimated Budget:** $25,000–$100,000+
- **Why Couples Love It:**
    - **Ultimate privacy:** Elite private overwater bungalows and single-resort islands away from the world.
    - **Unmatched aesthetics:** Glowing white sandbanks, vibrant coral lagoons, and beautiful underwater venue possibilities.
    - **Bespoke service:** Tailor-made itineraries, private luxury yachts, and personal butler care for a VIP experience.

### 10. Fiji

Fiji is ideal for couples looking for a deeply laid-back island atmosphere, authentic connection, and intimate celebrations.

- **Best Season:** May–October
- **Estimated Budget:** $12,000–$45,000+
- **Why Couples Love It:**
    - **The "Bula" spirit:** Warm, welcoming locals who celebrate your union with traditional South Pacific music and ceremonies.
    - **Untouched natural beauty:** Quiet, uncrowded islands wrapped in palm trees, offering authentic isolation.
    - **Perfect for elopements:** Exceptionally friendly regulations and packages for small, intimate micro-weddings.

### Final thoughts

Whether you're looking for a luxury celebration in Santorini, a tropical escape in Bali, or a romantic vineyard wedding in Tuscany, there's a destination wedding location to match every vision and budget.

The key is finding a destination that reflects your personality and creates unforgettable memories for both you and your guests.
